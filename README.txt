The contained file, chromatinBoundaryIntensity, is a matlab script that was used for the intensity measurements of nuclear envelope signal. It takes in two images, an intensity image of the desired signal (in our work, Emerin), and a mask of the nucleus, generated from a nuclear stain, and outputs the mean fluorescence intensity of the desired signal surrounding the bounndary over time.
